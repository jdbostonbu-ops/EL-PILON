04 / ABOUT

ABOUT US.

EL PILÓN BAR & RESTAURANT is a popular Dominican culinary establishment located in New London, CT. IT IS KNOWN FOR SERVING AUTHENTIC, HOME-COOKED HISPANIC COMFORT FOOD IN A COZY, CASUAL NEIGHBORHOOD ATMOSPHERE, AND AVERAGES AN IMPRESSIVE 4.3-STAR RATING.

GUESTS CAN ORDER FOOD AHEAD OR TAKE ADVANTAGE OF STANDARD TABLE SERVICE. POPULAR ITEMS FROM THE EL PILÓN ONLINE MENU INCLUDE:

  • El Plato del Pilón (Pilón Plate) — a hearty house specialty dish.
  • Mangú con los Tres Golpes — traditional Dominican mashed plantains paired with fried eggs, salami, and cheese.
  • Bistec Encebollado — savory steak cooked with onions in a wine reduction.
  • Mofongo De Camarones — Mashed plantains combined with garlic and pork rinds, topped with sautéed shrimp in a creamy garlic sauce, garnished with fresh herbs.
